About

Samantha Sparkles

 

 

I worked in a primary school for 5 years, and whilst I enjoyed helping children learn, the thing that made my heart happiest, was being able to make them laugh, and help them feel valued and special.

So I created Samantha Sparkles , where I do just that, by offering Princess appearances and party packages, overflowing with fun games and activities, music, bubbles and lots of sparkle.

Meet and Mingle

Want to add that extra bit of magic to your child's party, and make their dreams come true by meeting their favourite princess?! Then a meet and mingle is for you! Your chosen princess will come to your party, and take everyone on a story tale adventure into her land. She'll give out plenty of hugs, present the cake, and sing Happy Birthday (which also includes a glitzy wish). She'll pose for photos, and then be on her sparkly way.

Parties
Want all that meet and mingle magic to last a little longer....?! Then book a party!
Your 90 minute party will include everything that's in the meet and greet, plus 1hr of super fun games and activities in your chosen princess theme, and lots of music and dancing. It also includes bubbles, and a special little gift for the birthday star.
